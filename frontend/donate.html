How Your Donation Helps

Our streamlined process ensures your surplus food reaches those in need, quickly and safely.

1. You Schedule

Fill out the form with details about the food items and choose a convenient pickup time.

2. We Collect

A verified FoodBridge volunteer is dispatched to your location for a timely pickup.

3. We Distribute

The food is delivered to our network of partner NGOs and community kitchens.
