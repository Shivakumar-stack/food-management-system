Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FoodBridge.

How fast can a donation be picked up?

Most urban requests are matched in 30 to 60 minutes based on volunteer capacity and proximity.

What food types are accepted?

Prepared meals and packaged surplus can be listed with quantity, storage conditions, and expiry details.

Do volunteers receive route support?

Yes. Volunteers get suggested routes, pickup priorities, and delivery checkpoints in one flow.

Can companies use this for CSR reporting?

Every completed donation is logged so teams can track outcomes, compliance, and reporting metrics.

How do NGOs get onboarded?

Create an organization account, complete verification, and begin receiving match-ready requests.

Is there a cost to use FoodBridge?

FoodBridge is a non-profit and free to use for all participants. We are supported by grants and public donations.
