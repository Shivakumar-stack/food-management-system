Terms of Service

Rules, responsibilities, and platform usage expectations for all FoodBridge participants.

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Eligibility & Accounts

Users must provide accurate registration details and keep credentials secure. Organization accounts must be authorized representatives.

Platform Conduct

Users agree to lawful, respectful use of the platform and must not manipulate availability, claim statuses, or operational data.

Donation Operations

Donors, volunteers, and NGOs are responsible for the accuracy of logistics details, food handling compliance, and timely status updates.

Suspension & Termination

FoodBridge may suspend or terminate accounts for abuse, fraud, repeated policy violations, or misuse of platform resources.

Liability & Updates

Service availability may evolve over time. We reserve the right to update terms with notice through published policy updates.
