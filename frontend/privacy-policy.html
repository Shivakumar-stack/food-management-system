Privacy Policy

How FoodBridge collects, uses, secures, and protects your information across platform workflows.

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Information We Collect

We collect account details, profile information, platform usage data, and donation workflow records needed to operate FoodBridge services.

How We Use Information

Data is used to match donations, coordinate pickups and claims, improve reliability, prevent fraud, and provide support. We do not sell personal data.

Data Sharing

Operational data is shared only with authorized platform participants and service providers that support core logistics, communication, and security operations.

Security & Retention

We apply access controls, encrypted transport, audit logging, and retention limits to protect records and comply with legal obligations.

Your Rights

You may request profile corrections, account deletion, and data export where applicable by contacting our support team at hello@foodbridge.org.
