Volunteer Roles

How You Can Help

Choose the role that fits your skills and availability

Food Pickup Driver

Collect surplus food, verify packaging at pickup, and confirm drop-off in the app.

  • Valid driver's license
  • Own vehicle (2/4 wheeler)
  • Flexible schedule
  • Smartphone for navigation
Apply as Driver

Operations Coordinator

Coordinate pickup windows, assign routes, and verify handoff logs for compliance.

  • Good communication skills
  • Basic computer knowledge
  • Problem-solving ability
  • 4-6 hours/week commitment
Apply as Coordinator

Distribution Helper

Support NGOs with sorting, portioning, and safe distribution to beneficiaries.

  • Compassionate nature
  • Physical ability to lift boxes
  • Weekend availability
  • Team player attitude
Apply as Helper
